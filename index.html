About Cake House


The Cake House built in 2026 is a modern dessert boutique designed with a warm and welcoming atmosphere, featuring soft pastel colors, curved walls, and large glass panels that display beautifully decorated cakes from outside. Inside, the shop blends futuristic design with cozy seating, making it a comfortable place for families and friends to relax and enjoy desserts together. The bakery showcases handcrafted pastries, signature layered cakes, and seasonal specialty treats, all prepared using smart kitchen technology introduced in 2026 to ensure precision, freshness, and consistency. Customers can also watch expert bakers decorate cakes live, creating an interactive and engaging experience. Alongside the bakery area, there is a small café corner where visitors can enjoy fresh cakes with coffee and tea. Built with eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient lighting, the Cake House reflects a commitment to sustainability while maintaining elegance and style. Since its opening, it has become a local landmark known for creativity, quality ingredients, and beautifully customized cake designs that make every celebration special.

About Slice Cake
Slice cake became popular in the early 1900s when bakers began cutting whole cakes into individual portions for easier serving. As cafés and bakeries expanded, sliced cakes allowed people to enjoy different flavors without buying an entire cake. After World War II, improved kitchen appliances and affordable ingredients helped slice cake spread into homes and celebrations. By the late 20th century, layered and cream-filled slice cakes gained worldwide popularity across many cultures. Today, slice cake is enjoyed globally and represents convenience, sharing, and creativity in modern baking.
